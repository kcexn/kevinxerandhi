Freycinet Lodge

Overlooking the blue waters of Great Oyster Bay and with the rugged Hazards mountain range as your backdrop, the location of Freycinet Lodge's accommodation in Freycinet National Park is second to none.

Freycinet Lodge is a beautiful lodge located just past Coles Bay, Tasmania. The lodge has offered a discount to any guest of ours that chooses to stay in the accomodation the lodge offers. If you're from outside of Tasmania we recommend staying at the lodge.
